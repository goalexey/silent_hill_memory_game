Save the characters from being trapped inside Silent Hill.


Once you start the game, the cells will reveal the location of each character briefly before hiding them again. Your goal is to memorize the location of each character and "save" them by selecting the appropriate cell

Be aware of monsters and especially Pyramid Head. Choosing the cell with a monster will result in your death

You have to save each character and avoid all the monsters to win the game
